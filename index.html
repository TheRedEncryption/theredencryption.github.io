FROM GAMER TO GAME DEVELOPER

Ever since I was a child, I've always been playing video games in my free time. I started, as many kids did, with Scratch, making a multitude of FnAF-inspired games, then moving on to Roblox Studio (Lua), Godot (GDScript), and Unity (C#).
